# Code Of Conduct

Be direct, technical, and respectful. Assume contributors are trying to improve
the project, and keep disagreement focused on evidence, contracts, and user
impact.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxxing, spam, and repeated bad-faith disruption.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate
this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer through GitHub.
